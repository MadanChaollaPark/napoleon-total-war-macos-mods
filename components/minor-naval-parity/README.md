# WW0 minor naval parity and Spanish drydock repair

This standalone Mod-type pack repairs the verified late-navy gaps exposed when
WW0 makes AI and side-campaign factions playable.

It adds 42 faction-specific permissions for the existing generic Carronade and
steam ships:

- Belgium, Netherlands and United Netherlands: Steam Frigate and Ironclad.
- Denmark, Sweden, Norway and Portugal: all four generic steam ships.
- Papal States, Venice, Courland and Crimea: Carronade Frigate plus all four
  generic steam ships.

It also connects Spain's four national steam ships to
`pNavySpain3_steam_drydock`, makes the Spanish Steam Frigate require Steam Ship
Propulsion, and makes the Spanish steam drydock require Steam Engine.

The pack does not grant technologies, buildings or ships for free. Existing
technology, building, model and crew records still apply.
