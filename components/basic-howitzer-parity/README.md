# WW0 basic Howitzer parity

This standalone pack gives Spain, Portugal, Switzerland and Crimea their own
faction-keyed 7-lber Howitzer rows in the normal Great Arsenal, Ordnance Board
and Engineer School chain.

Each faction receives a new, uniquely keyed uniform mapping that reuses its own
existing artillery appearance and exact RGB colour record. Donor uniform keys
are not overwritten.

The faction-keyed unit rows also point to a national artillery icon and
information card already present in Napoleon's `data.pack`. The pack does not
copy or redistribute those images. Spain uses its rebel 7-lber Howitzer card
and Crimea uses the Ottoman Howitzer card. Napoleon does not ship national
Howitzer cards for Portugal or Switzerland, so those two use their national
9-lber and 6-lber artillery cards. The unit in battle remains the normal
7-lber Howitzer with the faction's own artillery uniform.

This component is generated deterministically by `source/build_pack.py`.
