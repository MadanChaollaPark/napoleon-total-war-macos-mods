# Third-party notices

This repository contains only original compatibility scripts, small database
submods, and a compact binary delta. It does not redistribute Napoleon: Total
War, World War zerO, or Radious Campaign AI.

## Napoleon: Total War

Napoleon: Total War and its data formats are property of Creative Assembly and
SEGA. The macOS port is published by Feral Interactive. This project is an
unofficial fan project and is not affiliated with or endorsed by those parties.

## World War zerO

[World War zerO](https://www.moddb.com/mods/world-war-zero) is created by
Gromazeka. It is a required base for the `ww0-*` components in this repository.
Its author permits use as a base or use of its developments with mandatory
attribution. The complete WW0 archive, packs, interface files, and startpos
files are not included here.

The startpos component contains only a bsdiff delta against one checksummed WW0
Europe startpos. Users must supply their own installed copy of WW0.

## Radious Campaign AI

[Radious Campaign AI](https://www.moddb.com/games/napoleon-total-war/downloads/radious-campaign-ai)
is credited by ModDB to Radious / Sir Thoragoros. It remains an optional,
separately downloaded dependency. Neither its RAR archive nor its pack is
included in this repository.
